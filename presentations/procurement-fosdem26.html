Open Source isn't a Free Chair. It's a Free Puppy.

Maintenance = food, training, vet bills. Drive home ongoing costs; procurement needs to fund upkeep.

The Contract Hack

Mandatory Requirement: Vendor must demonstrate history of upstream contribution.
All bug fixes must be submitted upstream.

Reference: Drupal Association RFP Guide

Explain why upstream-first clauses reduce forked risk and improve velocity.

DITAP & Procurement Culture

  • Procurement officers fear risk
  • Open standards reduce lock-in risk
  • Resource: USDS DITAP curriculum
Invite procurement teams to DITAP; stress risk framing and open standards.
