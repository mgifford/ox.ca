Hack Your RFP (Side A)

  • Contribution history: Vendor must show upstream commits.
  • Upstream first: All fixes must be submitted upstream.
  • Standards: Must support WCAG 2.2 AA and OpenACR.

CFO Whisperer (Side B)

The analogy: "It is not a chair; it is a puppy." Ongoing care is the cost.

ModelCost PatternOutcome
Proprietary rentRecurring license OpExLock-in risk
FLOSS (unfunded)0% upstreamSecurity + supply-chain risk
FLOSS (sustained)1–5% upstreamResilience and velocity

The 1–5% rule: Allocate a slice of contract value to upstream sustainment.
